We will need a little notation. Let
\begin{align*} P_1(H) \amp = \mbox{ the probability that Player 1 plays H; }\\ P_1(T) \amp = \mbox{ the probability that Player 1 plays T;}\\ P_2(H) \amp = \mbox{ the probability that Player 2 plays H; }\\ P_2(T) \amp = \mbox{ the probability that Player 2 plays T.} \end{align*}
Also, we will let \(E_1(H)\) be the expected value for Player 1 playing pure strategy H against a given strategy for Player 2. Similarly, \(E_2(H)\) will be Player 2's expected value for playing pure strategy H.
